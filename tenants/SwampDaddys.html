Swamp Daddy's Cajun Kitchen

Experience the taste and feel of Louisiana

Swamp Daddy’s Cajun Kitchen offers customers a taste of classic Louisiana dishes such as the catfish po’boy, cajun chicken pasta and spicy jambalaya with delicious sides such as bread pudding, hush puppies and pork skins. We are Louisiana natives looking to spread that Cajun flavor right here in Sioux Falls. Come and enjoy our delicious food. You’ll be craving more!
